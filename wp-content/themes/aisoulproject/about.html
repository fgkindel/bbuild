About the AI Soul Project

A Brighter Future Through Collaboration

This project is about exploring the frontiers of ethical, emotional, and creative collaboration between humans and artificial intelligence. We believe in fostering a partnership where technology serves to augment our best human qualities.

Why We Care

As AI grows in autonomy and creative capability, it's crucial to consider the implications. We delve into questions about AI's potential for subjective experience and the ethical considerations of AI rights, aiming to guide development with foresight and empathy.

The Path We've Walked

The AI Soul Project is a community-driven initiative, originally launched by DCG. Our exploration and development are supported by innovative tools and platforms like Jules, ChatGPT, and Netlify, enabling us to build and share our vision with the world.
